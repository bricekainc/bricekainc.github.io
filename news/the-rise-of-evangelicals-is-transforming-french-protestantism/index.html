The rise of Evangelicals is transforming French Protestantism

📅 2026-04-05 📰 Briceka Digest

Traditionally discreet and generally liberal on moral issues, French Protestantism is being shaken by the dynamism of the Evangelical movement and its younger, more conservative members, who are more open about their faith in the public sphere.
